🏏 Cricket Rules

Learn the basic rules of cricket in an easy and elegant way

📌 Basic Rules

  • A cricket match is played between two teams of 11 players each.
  • The game consists of batting, bowling, and fielding.
  • The team that scores more runs wins the match.

🏏 Scoring Runs

  • Batsmen score runs by running between the wickets or hitting boundaries.
  • 4 runs: Ball reaches the boundary along the ground.
  • 6 runs: Ball crosses the boundary in the air.

❌ Ways to Get Out

  • Bowled: The ball hits the stumps.
  • Caught: A fielder catches the ball before it touches the ground.
  • LBW: Ball hits the batsman's leg in front of the stumps.
  • Run Out: The batsman fails to reach the crease before the ball hits the stumps.

🎯 Fielding Rules

  • Each team has 11 fielders including a wicketkeeper.
  • Bowlers deliver overs of 6 balls each.
  • Fielding restrictions apply in powerplays.
