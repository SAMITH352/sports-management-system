📌 Basic Rules

  • A tennis match can be played as **singles** (one vs one) or **doubles** (two vs two).
  • The game starts with a **coin toss** to determine who serves first.
  • The server must serve the ball diagonally into the opponent’s service box.

🎾 Scoring System

  • Points are counted as: **Love (0), 15, 30, 40, Game**.
  • If both players reach **40-40**, it’s called **Deuce**, and a player must win by two points.
  • A player wins a **set** by winning at least **6 games** with a 2-game lead.

🎯 Serving Rules

  • Each player gets **two serves** per point.
  • If the first serve is a **fault**, they get a second serve.
  • If the ball hits the net but lands in the correct service box, it’s a **let**, and the serve is retaken.

⚠️ Ball In & Out

  • The ball must land **within the court lines** to be valid.
  • If the ball touches the **line**, it is considered **in**.
  • A player loses a point if the ball goes **out of bounds** or fails to clear the net.

🏆 Tie-break Rules

  • If a set reaches **6-6**, a **tie-break** is played.
  • Players take turns serving after every **2 points**.
  • The first player to reach **7 points** with a 2-point lead wins the tie-break.
