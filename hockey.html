📌 Basic Rules

  • A hockey match consists of **three periods**, each lasting **20 minutes**.
  • Each team has **six players** on the ice at a time, including the **goalkeeper**.
  • The team that scores the most **goals** wins the match.

🥅 How to Score

  • A goal is scored when the puck **completely crosses the goal line**.
  • The puck must be played with a **stick**, and goals scored with a skate kick are **not allowed**.
  • Players cannot use their **hands** to direct the puck into the goal.

🚨 Penalties

  • Minor Penalty (2 minutes): Given for actions like tripping, holding, and high-sticking.
  • Major Penalty (5 minutes): Given for severe infractions like fighting.
  • Misconduct Penalty (10 minutes): Given for unsportsmanlike conduct.

⚡ Power Play & Penalty Kill

  • A **power play** occurs when one team has more players due to an opponent's penalty.
  • A **penalty kill** is when a team tries to defend while being short-handed.

⚠️ Icing & Offside

  • Icing: A team cannot shoot the puck across both red lines without an opponent touching it.
  • Offside: A player cannot enter the attacking zone before the puck.
