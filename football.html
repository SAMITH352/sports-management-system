📌 Basic Rules

  • A match consists of two halves, each lasting **45 minutes**.
  • Each team has **11 players**, including a goalkeeper.
  • The team that scores the most goals **wins the match**.

🥅 How to Score

  • A goal is scored when the ball **completely crosses the goal line**.
  • Only **legal body parts** (feet, head, chest) can be used to score.
  • Offside rules apply to prevent unfair advantages.

🚨 Fouls & Offenses

  • Handball: Players (except goalkeepers) cannot touch the ball with their hands.
  • Fouls: Tackling from behind, high kicks, and dangerous play result in free kicks or penalties.
  • Yellow & Red Cards: A yellow card is a warning; two yellows or a direct red results in a player being sent off.

🎯 Free Kicks & Penalties

  • **Direct free kicks** allow players to shoot at goal directly.
  • **Indirect free kicks** require another player to touch the ball before scoring.
  • **Penalty kicks** are awarded for fouls inside the penalty area.

⚠️ Offside Rule

  • A player is **offside** if they are nearer to the opponent’s goal than both the ball and the second-last defender when the ball is passed.
  • No offside offenses occur in their **own half** or during throw-ins, goal kicks, or corners.
